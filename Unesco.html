Ellora Caves

Aurangabd

The Ellora Caves complex is an extraordinary example of ancient Indian architecture and artistry, representing a harmonious blend of Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain religious traditions. It consists of 34 major caves, excavated from the basalt cliffs of the Charanandri Hills

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj terminus

Mumbai

Designed by British architect Frederick William Stevens, the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus was built in the late 19th century and completed in 1887.

Ajantha caves

Aurangabd

Located about 100 kilometres away from the historic city of Aurangabad, the Ajanta Caves are a cluster of approximately 30 rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments that consist of sanctuaries and monasteries dating back to the 2nd Century BC.

Victorian Gothic And Art Deco Ensembles of Mumbai

Mumbai

The Victorian Gothic and Art Deco Ensembles of Mumbai is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in the city of Mumbai, India. It consists of two distinct architectural styles that reflect different periods of Mumbai's history
